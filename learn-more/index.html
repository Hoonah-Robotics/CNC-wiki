When you learn more, you can do more¶

"Techniques, Trainings and Projects to powerup your CNC abilities"

You've completed the basic workflow! Now you can start exploring and creating more advanced projects. Here is a list of resources our team finds helpful for beginners.

eBooks¶

  • Getting Started with CNC
  • Foxalien Masuter Pro Manual

Software & Hardware Links¶

  • FoxAlien: The official site for our Masuter Pro. You can find manuals and machine specs here.
  • Easel: The CAM software we use. They have excellent tutorials and a "Projects" section with ideas.
  • Universal Gcode Sender (UGS): The official site for our control software.
  • Tinkercad: A great, free, simple-to-use CAD tool.
  • Inkscape: A powerful and free vector-graphics tool for making complex 2D designs (a great free alternative to Adobe Illustrator).
  • Ultimate CNC End Mill Guide for Beginners It's the End Mill that does all the cutting, so understanding how it works will guide you to more success in your projects!

Helpful YouTube Channels¶

  • Inventables: The makers of Easel. Their channel has hundreds of project ideas and tutorials.
  • Chris Powell (from the videos): The channel from the troubleshooting and setup videos. He has lots of great beginner CNC content.
  • Mark Lindsay CNC: A fantastic channel for learning the "why" behind CNC, including bits, feeds, and speeds.
  • IDC Woodcraft: Lots of practical CNC projects, often for hobbyist machines.

Project Ideas¶

  • CutRocket: A community site (run by Inventables) where people share their CNC project files, many of them for Easel.
  • Thingiverse: Mostly known for 3D printing, but you can find many .svg files and CNC-related projects here as well.
  • Tinkercad: Learn CNC With These 5 Projects: for CNC Routing, 3D Printing ...
