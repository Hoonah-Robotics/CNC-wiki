## When you learn more, you can do more
> "Techniques, Trainings and Projects to powerup your CNC abilities"

You've completed the basic workflow! Now you can start exploring and creating more advanced projects. Here is a list of resources our team finds helpful for beginners.

### Software & Hardware Links

* **[FoxAlien](https://www.foxalien.com):** The official site for our Masuter Pro. You can find manuals and machine specs here.
* **[Easel](https://easel.inventables.com):** The CAM software we use. They have excellent tutorials and a "Projects" section with ideas.
* **[Universal Gcode Sender (UGS)](https://winder.github.io/ugs_website/):** The official site for our control software.
* **[Tinkercad](https://www.tinkercad.com):** A great, free, simple-to-use CAD tool.
* **[Inkscape](https://inkscape.org/):** A powerful and **free** vector-graphics tool for making complex 2D designs (a great free alternative to Adobe Illustrator).
* **[Ultimate CNC End Mill Guide for Beginners](https://www.accu-cut.com.tw/en/news-detail/cnc-end-mill-guide/)** It's the End Mill that does all the cutting, so understanding how it works will guide you to more success in your projects!

### Helpful YouTube Channels

* **[Inventables](https://www.youtube.com/c/Inventables):** The makers of Easel. Their channel has hundreds of project ideas and tutorials.
* **[Chris Powell (from the videos)](https://www.youtube.com/@ChrisPowellMakes):** The channel from the troubleshooting and setup videos. He has lots of great beginner CNC content.
* **[Mark Lindsay CNC](https://www.youtube.com/@MarkLindsayCNC):** A fantastic channel for learning the "why" behind CNC, including bits, feeds, and speeds.
* **[IDC Woodcraft](https://www.youtube.com/@IDCWConnect):** Lots of practical CNC projects, often for hobbyist machines.

### Project Ideas

* **[CutRocket](https://cutrocket.com/):** A community site (run by Inventables) where people share their CNC project files, many of them for Easel.
* **[Thingiverse](https://www.thingiverse.com/):** Mostly known for 3D printing, but you can find many .svg files and CNC-related projects here as well.
